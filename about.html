Our Green Journey

Sustainable. Organic. Delicious.

From Farm to Bottle

Founded in 2020 by nutritionist Alex Green, Green Line began as a small farmers' market stand with a simple mission: to make healthy, organic juices accessible to everyone.

Today, we partner with 25+ local organic farms to source the freshest ingredients while supporting our community's agricultural ecosystem.

Our Core Values

100% Organic

We use only certified organic fruits and vegetables, free from pesticides and harmful chemicals.

Cold-Pressed

Our hydraulic press technology preserves maximum nutrients and enzymes.

Sustainability

From compostable bottles to solar-powered facilities, we minimize our environmental impact.

Community

We support local farmers and donate 5% of profits to nutrition education programs.

Meet Our Team

Alex Green

Founder & CEO

Certified nutritionist with 10+ years in the wellness industry.

Maria Rodriguez

Head Juicer

Master of flavor combinations and cold-press techniques.
